Welcome

This site is a community resource for the study and implementation of values in design (VID), reflecting a broad spectrum of theories, methodologies and disciplinary approaches. We invite contributions from researchers, practitioners, and public interest advocates. Please come back often and watch the site grow!  

Support for the development of this website was provided by the NSF for the NSF EAGER: Values in Design in the Future Internet Architecture, CNS/NetS 1058333 and the Intel Science and Technology Center for Social Computing.
